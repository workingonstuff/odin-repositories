Pad Thai

Description

Pad thai is delicious with a very rich flavor profile. This version is also super healthy.

I hate when recipes go on and on so let's just get to it.

Ingredients

  • Peanuts
  • Soy Sauce
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Stirfry vegetables that are approrpaite, like carrots, onions, etc.<
  2. Put in noodles, peanuts, and seasoning.
